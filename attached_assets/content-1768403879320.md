**Metrics**

# Consolidated insight.

The Dashboard brings together every signal Tree’d collects into a single, organized view. These metrics are presented clearly and always anonymously, giving museums a practical understanding of how visitors explore, learn, and move through their spaces.

- #### Visitor Behavior






  - Popular paths through the museum

  - Heatmap of gallery movement

  - Dwell time per visit and per artifact

  - Popularity of specific themes and collections


- #### Listening & Interaction






  - Audio track completion rates

  - Follow-up questions asked to the AI model

  - Question categories and themes

  - Number of scans per tour


- #### Language Mix






  - Language selection breakdown

  - Daily/weekly/monthly shifts

  - Popularity of certain artifacts, themes, and collections by each language


- #### System Performance






  - Number of Handsets currently in use

  - Battery levels

  - Charging status

  - Uptime and stability
